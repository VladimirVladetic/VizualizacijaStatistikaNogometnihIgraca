Welcome to Footballer Analysis Hub

On this website you can search to see how good your favorite players are in many aspects of play. This ranges from shot power, through speed, strength, all the way to goalkeeping attributes such as handling or distribution. You can compare up to 3 players using the radar chart section, search for the best players in a team based on the parameters you select using our scatter plot section and finally inspect each player in the dataset one by one in our individual player section, here you can see general information about the player as well as their best and worst 5 atribures.
